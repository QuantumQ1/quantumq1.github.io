# Conferences on Scattering Amplitudes and String Theory

This page collects recent and upcoming conferences and workshops related to **scattering amplitudes** and **string theory**.  
It may be useful for students and collaborators who want to follow the latest developments in the field.

---

## 1. Flagship Annual Conferences

### Amplitudes 2025 – Seoul National University

**Dates:** 16–20 June 2025  
**Location:** Seoul National University, Seoul, South Korea  

Amplitudes 2025 is the 17th installment of the annual **Amplitudes** conference series, bringing together researchers interested in formal and phenomenological aspects of scattering amplitudes, from pure mathematics to collider physics and gravitational waves.

- Official website (Indico):  
  <https://indico.global/event/13171/>  
- Info at IAS (Future Amplitudes conferences):  
  <https://www.ias.edu/sns/future-amplitudes-conferences>

---

### Strings 2025 – NYU Abu Dhabi

**Dates:** 6–10 January 2025  
**Location:** New York University Abu Dhabi, Abu Dhabi, UAE  

**Strings 2025** is the 35th edition of the flagship annual **Strings** conference, bringing together leading researchers in string theory, quantum gravity, and related areas of quantum field theory.

- Official website (NYUAD):  
  <https://nyuad.nyu.edu/en/events/2025/january/strings-2025-abu-dhabi.html>  
- Conference overview:  
  <https://nyuad.shorthandstories.com/strings-conference-abu-dhabi/>

---

### Strings 2024 – CERN (Past)

**Dates:** 3–7 June 2024  
**Location:** CERN and associated Swiss institutions  

Strings 2024 was hosted by CERN, EPFL, ETH Zürich, LAPTh, the University of Bern and the University of Geneva, and served as the 34th installment of the Strings series.

- Indico page and timetable:  
  <https://indico.cern.ch/event/1284995/>  

Even though this conference is in the past, the webpage and videos are a good resource for recent developments in string theory.

## 2. Selected Amplitudes-Related Meetings and Workshops

### Infrared Surprises of Scattering Amplitudes – CERN

**Dates:** 12–16 May 2025  
**Location:** CERN, Geneva, Switzerland  

A TH workshop focusing on infrared properties of scattering amplitudes in gauge theories and gravity, with connections to the S-matrix bootstrap and phenomenology.

- Indico page:  
  <https://indico.cern.ch/event/1461182/>

---

### California Amplitudes 2025 (UCLA Bhaumik Institute)

**Dates:** 8–9 November 2025  
**Location:** University of California, Los Angeles, USA  

A regional meeting devoted to scattering amplitudes and related topics, organized by the Bhaumik Institute.

- Conference info:  
  <https://bhaumik-institute.physics.ucla.edu/conferences-workshops>

---

### Michigan Interdisciplinary Meeting on Amplitudes: Bridges Between Physics and Mathematics

**Dates:** 24–26 October 2025  
**Location:** University of Michigan, Ann Arbor, USA  

An interdisciplinary conference aimed at bringing together physicists and mathematicians working on recent developments in scattering amplitudes.

- Event page:  
  <https://events.umich.edu/event/139780>

## 3. String-Theory-Related Conferences and Schools

### String-Math 2024 – ICTP, Trieste

**Dates:** 10–14 June 2024  
**Location:** ICTP, Trieste, Italy  

The **String-Math** series brings together mathematicians and physicists working on ideas related to string theory, emphasizing the mathematical structures behind the subject.

- Conference page:  
  <https://indico.ictp.it/event/10482>

---

### Iberian Strings 2024 – Porto

**Dates:** 17–19 January 2024  
**Location:** University of Porto, Portugal  

A regional string-theory conference focused on developments in string theory, AdS/CFT, quantum gravity and related topics.

- Indico page:  
  <https://indico.global/event/9031/>

---

### stringtheory.pl 2024 – Kraków

**Dates:** 7–8 June 2024  
**Location:** Kraków, Poland  

Part of the annual **stringtheory.pl** conference series, bringing together researchers interested in string theory and related topics, mainly from Poland and neighboring countries.

- Conference info:  
  <https://stringtheory.pl/stringtheory-pl-2024-2/>
